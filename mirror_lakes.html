About the Trail

Enjoy all nature has to offer in this magical landscape rife with mountain views, lakes, meadows, and wildlife! Spend a weekend camping here and enjoy activities like hiking, birdwatching, paddleboarding, and swimming. Seasonal activities include Huckleberry picking, fishing for Rainbow trout, and wildflower viewing. Pets welcome on a leash.
