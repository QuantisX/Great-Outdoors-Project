Tips & Safety

Before embarking on the Skyline Loop, make sure to be prepared for the trail's challenges. Here are some helpful tips:

  • Bring plenty of water and snacks.
  • Wear sturdy hiking boots with good grip.
  • Carry a map and know the trail markers.
  • Be mindful of wildlife, especially bears and other large animals.
  • Check weather conditions before you go and be prepared for changing weather.
  • Start early to avoid afternoon thunderstorms.
